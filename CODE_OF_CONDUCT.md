# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming space for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, behavior, and project goals.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Avoid harassment, insults, and personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or abusive language.
- Discriminatory comments or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants when behavior harms the project or its community.

To report a concern, email the address listed in `SECURITY.md`.
